What We Do?

We are an Infrastructure based private limited company working desperately for PMAY-HFA. We intend to change the archetype of people's lives by developing and evolving large scale affordable housing for both "Economically Weaker Section" (EWS) and "Lower Income Group" (LIG) units and also slowly targeting “Middle Income Group” (MIG) units.
DG is in receipt of work-order from Government PSUs for the construction of affordable housing projects at 8 prime locations of Rajasthan involving above 8000 LIG and EWS home units.
