    About Us

Welcome to Desert Greens Pvt. Ltd.


We are an Infrastructure based private limited company working desperately for PMAY-HFA. We intend to change the archetype of people's lives by developing and evolving large scale affordable housing for both "Economically Weaker Section" (EWS) and "Lower Income Group" (LIG) units and also slowly targeting “Middle Income Group” (MIG) units.

DG is in receipt of work-order from Government PSUs for the construction of affordable housing projects at 8 prime locations of Rajasthan involving above 8000 LIG and EWS home units.

Our Mission


The aim of the Desert Greens Pvt Ltd is to Make High-Quality Housing Affordable for Every Family by providing professional services which best meets the needs of our clients through working closely with them and their consultants, we shall find solutions to meet and exceed their requirements.

In all our activities, we shall respect and where possible, enhance the environment. Client satisfaction and job satisfaction for all employees are prime objectives of the Desert Greens Pvt Ltd.

Our Vision


The vision of Desert Greens Pvt Ltd. lies in the 3F’s rule “Forming, Framing and Finishing”.

We will tackle World’s Housing Crisis by Designing and Delivering One Million Homes with a Price and Amenities Within the Reach of all.

We shall resourcefully address all types of projects and apply the highest standard of workmanship and integrity in all aspects of performance.

Our collaborative approach through early design involvement, engineering expertise and integrated delivery ensures that we can complete complex projects in exact time, cost and quality standards, safely and sustainably.
