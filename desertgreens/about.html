    About Us

Welcome to Desert Greens Pvt. Ltd.


We are an Infrastructure based private limited company working desperately for PMAY-HFA. We intend to change the archetype of people's lives by developing and evolving large scale affordable housing for both "Economically Weaker Section" (EWS) and "Lower Income Group" (LIG) units and also slowly targeting “Middle Income Group” (MIG) units.

DG is in receipt of work-order from Government PSUs for the construction of affordable housing projects at 8 prime locations of Rajasthan involving above 8000 LIG and EWS home units.


Our Mission


Our mission is to attain client satisfaction by building sustainable environments by enabling right people to capitalize conceptual plans, resources and digital technologies and providing solution across total construction life cycle for the clients and generating maximum employment for people and value for its stakeholders.

We are committed to having a long-lasting impact on our residents. Our work demonstrates that we are committed to addressing the root causes of poverty and homelessness.
